Announcing the Gerald Sanders Memorial Voucher The Gerald Sanders Memorial Voucher for Transcendence in Turbulence is an award bestowed on a composer deemed thoughouly confusing by the New Confusion board of directors. The voucher was concieved by the late Gerald Sanders as a way to get composers exited about the new confusion movement. In his memory, I, Barnibus Appleton, we award a voucher to the composer who can sew the most chaos with their score. The first Call for Confusion will progress as follows: Guidelines
  • Your piece should be neither clear nor straightforward
  • If your score is text based, it is better to have more words than necessary
  • Works of all instrumentations will be accepted, although works including viola will have a chance to receive the voucher
  • If a work has never been performed, it will have a 25% chance of being juried. If it has been performed more than 1 time, but less than 3 times, it will have a 75% chance of being judged. If the piece has been performed over 10 times please do not submit to this call for confusion.
  • If electronics are used in a work, the composer must provide all of the equipment and also backup equipment. This includes speakers, cables, laptops, interfaces, microphones, microphone stands, microphone clips, and generators.
  • Previous winners of any sort of voucher will be considered equally to non-voucher recipients.
  • Composers may submit up to 7 works, but only 5 will be considered for the voucher. Only one voucher will be awarded per catagory. There is one category.
    • Requirements
  • You must inlcude your first name (only) on every 3rd page of your score.
  • Your name may appear on the title page of the score, but may not appear on any other page of the score.
  • You may submit a MIDI file, but this will disqualify you from the voucher for the next decade.
  • If your composition was composed before 2018 it is not eligable for submission unless it is for 7 or more instruments, then it must have been composed after 2006.
  • Composers of all ages are welcome!
  • A signed statment that allows for the recording, performance, and decimation of the work by Barnibus Appleton free of charge. Pieces that transfer complete ownership will have a better chance of winning this voucher.
  • Theatrical Elements
  • The text "In Memorium of Gerald Sanders" must be incorperated into the composition is some way. It also must appear on the 3rd page.
    • About the New Confusion Ensemble The New Confusion Ensemble is a modular group of performers that regularly features viola, hyperbass flute, citole, finger cymbals, and fixed electronics. As the director Barnibus Appleton, disperses the New Confusion Ensemble away from his guidance and allows the performers to experience true confusion through conflicting instruction, arbitrary and intermitant conducting, and inconsistant rehearsal times. The New Confusion Ensemble is never the same for any rehearsal. This is by design to maximize player confusion.
